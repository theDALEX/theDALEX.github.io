Student Square (University Team Project)

It simplifies the process of finding roommates and discovering peers with similar interests, making student life more connected and enjoyable. With its intuitive design and engaging features, Student Square empowers students to build meaningful relationships within their academic communities

Tank Wars (University Project)

Tank Wars is a dynamic and action-packed shooting game developed during my first-year programming course using Processing. The game features a thrilling battle environment where players control tanks, aiming and firing to outmaneuver enemies, With its simple yet addictive mechanics, 'Tank Wars' delivers an exciting experience as players strategize their shots to dominate the battlefield. This project showcases the application of fundamental programming concepts and introduces interactive gameplay with a focus on responsiveness and user engagement

TECH STACK: Processing
